Our Story

Education Rooted in
Indian Values

Established in 1952 under the banner of Vidya Bharati, Saraswati Shishu Vidya Mandir has been a beacon of holistic education for over seven decades. Situated at the heart of College Square, Cuttack, we blend academic rigour with cultural values to nurture complete human beings.

📚

Holistic Curriculum

Academics blended with yoga, Sanskrit, and cultural programmes for complete development.

🌳

Value-Based Education

Rooted in the philosophy of Vidya Bharati — nurturing character alongside knowledge.

🔬

Modern Infrastructure

Smart classrooms, well-equipped science labs, digital library, and sports facilities.

🏅

Consistent Academic Excellence

98%+ board results year after year with multiple district toppers.

What We Offer

Academic Programmes

From Nursery to Class XII, we offer a structured, comprehensive curriculum under the Odisha Board with Science, Commerce and Humanities streams.

🌱

Pre-Primary (Nursery–KG)

Play-based, activity-rich early learning designed to spark curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning from the very start.

Ages 3–5
📖

Primary School (Class I–V)

Building the foundation in Language, Mathematics, Science and Social Studies with emphasis on experiential learning.

Ages 6–10
🔭

Middle School (Class VI–VIII)

Deepening conceptual understanding across subjects, with introduction to Hindi, Sanskrit and computer literacy.

Ages 11–13
📐

Secondary (Class IX–X)

Odisha Board Matriculation preparation with expert coaching, model exams, and personalised mentorship.

Board Exams

Science Stream (XI–XII)

PCM and PCB tracks with JEE/NEET oriented teaching, dedicated labs, and experienced science faculty.

📊

Commerce & Humanities (XI–XII)
